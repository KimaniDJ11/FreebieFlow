FreebieFlow: App Support

This is the official support page for FreebieFlow, a SwiftUI app that tracks services offering free credits or trials. It reminds you when renewals are due and keeps your "used" vs "active" services organized.

Contact

Email: support@freebieflow.app

FAQ

Q: What is FreebieFlow?
A: FreebieFlow is a SwiftUI app that tracks services offering free credits or trials. It reminds you when renewals are due and keeps your "used" vs "active" services organized.

Q: How do I add a new service?
A: Tap Add Service in the toolbar, fill in the service name, associated email, number of free credits, and the renewal date, then hit Save.

Q: Can I edit or delete a service later?
A: Yes. Open the service from the list, choose Edit, adjust the details, and save. Swipe left (or use context menus) to delete entries you no longer need.

Q: What does the "Used" toggle do?
A: Marking a service as "Used" moves it to the Used section so you can focus on remaining freebies. When the renewal date passes, FreebieFlow automatically resets it to Active with the next renewal date.

Q: How do I import data from the web app?
A: Export the JSON file from the web version, open FreebieFlow, and use Import Data from the toolbar. The app validates entries and saves successful imports.
