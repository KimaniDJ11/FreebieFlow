FreebieFlow: App Support

This is the official support page for FreebieFlow, your app for managing and tracking free recurring credits from online services.

Contact

Email: support@freebieflow.app

FAQ

Q: What does FreebieFlow do?
A: It helps you keep track of monthly/weekly free credits/allowances from online platforms so you never lose value.

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Go to app settings and tap "Reset Password." For issues, contact us using the email above.

Q: Something isn’t working—how can I get help?
A: Email us and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible (most replies in 1-2 business days).
